This tool will check folders of your choosing for mp3 files from this playlist.
This is done to automate the download of songs you are missing.
Searching Folders is not as compliant with mobile devices. Instead Just Download All.
Choose a folder then click "Search". You can repeat with many folders.
When you are ready, "Download" will download a zip file of all the songs you are missing.

After you download the songs with the tool, follow these instructions.

1. Open Spotify, Click on your Profile Picture, Click Settings.

2. Look for "Your Library" or "Local Files" Header.

3. Enable "Show Local Files" or "Show Audio from this Device"

4. If you have not, extract the zipped download into a folder of you choosing. Remember that folder path.

5. (On PC Spotify) In Settings look for "Show Songs From" Header.

6. (On PC Spotify) Click "Add a Source" then point that to the folder from step 4.

7. In Your Library you should now have "Local Files" available. If not, Restart Spotify.

8. Go to my Spotify Playlist that you downloaded for. (A link to my Spotify is in the header of the site)

9. Click Download. You are now done, Enjoy!
