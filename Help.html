How it Works

First of all, this is a tool to download Mp3 files of songs that are not originally on Spotify that I have placed in my playlists.

If there is a song on Spotify that is greyed out, it is either not available anymore, or is a Local Song that has been put in that playlist.

In my playlists, they are local songs. This tool downloads them, puts them in a folder, and shows you how to make Spotify recognize them so that you can play them.

Web Spotify, Alexa, Chromecast and other casting tool CANNOT play local files
(As far as I know)

By pressing on "Playlists" in the navigation bar, you can get started and pick a playlist you'd like to retrieve songs for.

Instructions will be included along the way.
